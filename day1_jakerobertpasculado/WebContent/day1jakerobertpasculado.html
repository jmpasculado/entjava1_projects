Chapter 4 Evening
The person that was in front of Arnold was a girl named Fiona, she was a clinic doctor from a far town that was over ran by bandits. They agreed on moving out of the house to search for others, with a bag of weapons, ammo, , rations and medical supplies they were ready to search on the dangers of Pandora during the long night.
